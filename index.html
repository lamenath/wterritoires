What is wterritoires?

wterritoires is a social web application for people, projects and organizations involved in Rural Development.

Political objectives

  • Ensure people’s cooperation
  • Project stakeholders‘ access to UE RD funds information
  • Spending funds and reduce axes spending disparities

Organization objectives

  • Organizing the network (individuals, organizations, authorities, projects, events, resources, skills, industries, etc.)
  • Decompartmentalization
  • Acceleration of information exchange
  • “Capitalization” / experience and good practices sharing / document organization

Who is it for?

This app is designed for all scales of projects stakeholders:

  • Geographic/administrative scale: township, town community, Pays (in France: land that includes several town communities), département, région, inter-regional, international
  • Individual or Collective management team or ownership of the project: individual driven project, organisation driven project (company, local association, regional association, national association)
  • Project status: idea, prefiguration, fundraising, work in progress, achieved
  • Topic: from a very specific topic (ex. domestic bees impact on wild flora) to a wider topic (EAFRD in FR-Picardy, wood industry in FR-Picardy, Pôle d'excellence rurale).

Features

Project or Event Collaborative Space Features:

  • Project presentation « project postcard »
  • Activity feed
  • members list and direct messaging (stakeholders, contributors, observers)
  • documents, photo, video, audio resources
  • Forum
